# Topia U Decide

**A strategy game of transformation. 60 days. No narrator. Your choices.**

---

You have just signed the order.

Every adult in the country receives a new currency — *Americash* — that expires in sixty days unless spent. Rent is eliminated. Healthcare is free. The extraction layer has been removed from the food supply. The accountability tribunals are convening.

Something is happening. Whether it becomes something *livable* — or something else entirely — depends on the decisions you make.

**There is no correct ending. There is only the world you build.**

---

## How It Plays

*Topia U Decide* compresses a 60-day political and economic transformation into ~12 critical turning points. At each moment, you face a real decision with real consequences.

- A currency system that could circulate or collapse
- Tribunals that could deliver justice or become instruments
- A media landscape you can shape or lose
- Community organizations that could govern or fracture
- International pressure that doesn't wait for you to be ready

Each choice shifts the world. Stability erodes or compounds. The clock extends with coherent decisions and bleeds with contradictory ones. Run out of time and the transformation fails — hard fail, restart.

The ending isn't a score. It's a description of the world you made.

---

## What It Teaches

The game doesn't lecture. The mechanics do the work.

Players discover *why* things work the way they do by making them fail. Ignore the narrative on Day 30 and watch the counter-movement win the frame war. Coerce instead of organize and watch the resentment compound. Rush without infrastructure and watch the momentum die.

The lesson is in the consequence. The game trusts the player to find it.

---

## The World

*Topia U Decide* is set in a near-future United States in 2029. The world was built from the ground up — its currency mechanics, its accountability systems, its cooperative infrastructure — and is presented without explanation or apology. Players new to the world learn it through play. Players who know the source novel find the deeper architecture underneath.

The game is a standalone experience. No prior knowledge required.

---

## Design

- **Mobile-first** — designed for touch, playable anywhere
- **~15 minutes** for a full run
- **Hard fail** — the transformation can collapse; restart and try differently
- **6 distinct endings** — Utopian, Dystopian, and the complicated territory between
- **Minimal HUD** — a few numbers always visible, full depth one tap away
- **No narrator** — the mechanics speak

Single HTML file. No dependencies. No server. No data collected.

## About

*Topia U Decide* is a companion game to the novel *The 60-Day Liberation*.

The novel follows a 60-day transformation of the United States through the eyes of a sovereign AI observer, the people living through the change, and the forces trying to stop it. The game puts you in the seat of the administration making the decisions the novel depicts.

They are not always the same decisions.
